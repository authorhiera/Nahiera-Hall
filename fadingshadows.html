Fading Shadows

The story centers on Evan, a reserved artist grappling with depression after a family tragedy, and his developing relationship with Tyler, a popular student drawn to Evan's anonymous artwork. Their budding romance faces challenges as Evan deals with self-worth issues and the fear of burdening Tyler with his struggles.
